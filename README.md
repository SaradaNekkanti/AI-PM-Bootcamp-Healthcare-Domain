# 🏥 AI Product Manager Bootcamp (Healthcare Domain)

Welcome! This repo is a curated 2-month hands-on roadmap for Product Managers to pivot into AI Product Management with a focus on Healthcare.

## 📅 Weekly Topics

- **Week 1:** AI/ML Foundations for PMs (**with Healthcare datasets**)
- **Week 2:** LLMs and Real-World Integrations (**prompting, APIs, HuggingFace**)
- **Week 3:** AI Product Management (**design, regulation, actual project build**)
- **Week 4:** AI Evaluations & Responsible AI (**healthcare risk, safety, bias**)

Each day involves **~2hr of learning/practical work** | **All resources are free or have free tiers**

## 💼 Build Your Portfolio in 1 Day

Templates and guides included to launch your first AI healthcare case study/demo as a GitHub & LinkedIn project.

---

## 📂 Structure

- `Week1_Foundations/` - AI, ML, Data, Healthcare intro
- `Week2_LLM_Integrations/` - LLMs, APIs, prompt engineering
- `Week3_Building_AI_Healthcare_Products/` - Product skills, privacy, cases
- `Week4_AI_Evaluations_Safety/` - Eval, risk, AI safety
- `Portfolio/` - Guidance to publish your own AI PM project

## 👩‍💻 Getting Started

Pick any week folder and follow the daily plan. Fork this repo, add your notes, and document your hands-on progress for recruiters!
